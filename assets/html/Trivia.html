28 Lingo

Popular terms used in the game in Kerala and its possible origins. (This are strictly opinions of the author and have no research or endorsements supporting it, added here for the entertainment of curious minds)

Perise: Top active card in a suit. This term does seem to have some Tamil origin as Perise in Tamil means big which is what these cards are when it referred this way.

Gulan: Jack , likely originated from Ghulam , meaning servant in Bengali/Arabic. Jack is considered as the servant among the court cards like King and Queen in a deck of cards.

Thani: A special bid where the player claims to win all tricks by playing solo. This term likely came from the similar option in the related game Thunee

Cot: The claim made in the middle of game that bidding team will win all tricks. This likely originated from the “Full Court” also used as “Court” sounding like Cot, from a variant of 304 game where it is used for similar action.

Thani and Cot are not supported in this iOS app as the additional complexity do not justify the marginal fun (strictly author’s opinion)

Kunuk: The token ornament worn by losing team in classic real world game . It is very striking that there is a term "Khanuck" in Thunee , although it is used in different context ( more close to Cot).
