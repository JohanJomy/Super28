• Quick Play

    Any time during the play of cards, you can quickly end the current game by pressing the  button or swiping table from left to right. This is like dropping your hand in traditional game, but in this case the app will automatically play your hand till end and show the end result immediately. This option is not avaialbe for Card Count Match.

  • Quick (auto) Bid

    During bidding, you can quickly end the bid (auto bid all unbid hands) by pressing the  button or swiping table from left to right. Trump will be set automatically by this action. This option is not avaialbe for Card Count Match.

  • Quick Deal

    If deal animations are making you impatient or slowing you down, you can quickly finish a deal by swiping the hand area or table while deal is in progress.

  • Rearrange Cards in Hand

    You can change card order in your hand by swiping the hand area to left or to right to shift cards in the respective direction.
