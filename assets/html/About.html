Twenty-Eight

This app plays the Six Player variant of the popular card game Twenty-Eight. You play one hand and your iOS device will play for other five hands. This is a popular card game in KERALA, India and belongs to the Jass group of point-trick card games. Similar games include the game 29 (popular in Bangladesh and parts of North India) and the game 304 (popular in Sri Lanka and parts of South India).

Origins of the 28 Game

Most likely the game originated from the game 304 , the popular card game in Sri Lanka. It is very close to Thunee which is also a descendent of 304. Also 28 is almost identical to 29 except for minor scoring rules.
